\section{Objectives}

The primary objective of this initiative is to create an online store for the global carpet industry called CARPET, which will be a web-based platform tailored to the industry's needs. \\
Carpet online store is designed to streamline and accelerate the process of connecting carpet customers and producers while also facilitating collaboration and communication among industry professionals. \\
The platform aims to equip carpet producers and suppliers with a comprehensive tool to showcase their products, manage orders, and monitor customer feedback. \\
At the same time, it provides customers with an intuitive interface for browsing, comparing, and purchasing carpets from a diverse range of suppliers worldwide. \\
Customers can also chat with producers online, and there is the possibility of bargaining for the purchase of the carpet between the customer and the producer.
