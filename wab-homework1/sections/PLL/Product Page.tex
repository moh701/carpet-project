On this website, users can view and purchase the product by going to the product page. The page is divided into two sections. On the left side there are images of the product, while on the right side there are details of the product, including the title, description and price.\\\\
To purchase the product, the user can specify the quantity of the product and click "Add to Cart". The product will then be added to the user's cart and the user can proceed to checkout to complete the purchase.
\hfill \break
\begin{figure}[h]
    \centering
    \includegraphics[scale=0.3]{logos/Images/pages/Product Page - Add To Cart.png}
    \caption{Product Page - Add To Cart}
    \label{fig2:my_label}
\end{figure}
\hfill \break
If the user wants to make a counter offer, he can click on the "Make offer" button. A new window will open where the user can explain and justify the offer. The seller is then informed about the offer and can either accept or reject it.
\newpage
\begin{figure}[h]
    \centering

    \caption{Product Page - Make An Offer}
    \label{fig3:my_label}
\end{figure}
\hfill \break
At the bottom of the page there are also reviews about the product. Users can read reviews and submit their own reviews to help other users decide whether to buy the product or not.\\\\
Overall, this website provides an easy and reliable way to buy products and make counter offers. The use of images and product information ensures that users have all the information they need to make an informed decision. By being able to post and read reviews, users can also benefit from other users' experiences and choose the best product for their needs.
